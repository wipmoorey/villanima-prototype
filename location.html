Kefalonia, Greece

Kefalonia is a beautiful island of contrasts with its variety of ancient ruins, which run alongside the famous beaches, glorious forests, mountain ranges and golden sands.

The Island also has many picturesque villages scattered all around its mountainous area, plains and its beautiful coastline. Look to visit the varied places of interest and have fun exploring the island.

Do not miss the town of Argostoli, Monastery of Panagia Vlachernon, the fortress of St George, the beautiful beach of Lourda, Ratzakli, Sami, Agia Efimia, Assos, the beautiful beach of Mirtos, the lake of Melisani, the cave of Drogorati and many others.

All of the beauty and interest spots on the island can be easily reached by car. You can also take advantage of the natural beauty spots, and take your pick of the beautiful landscape and natural beaches as you travel around.

The mountain areas and natural parks are as nature made them in stunning fashion and take you into the heart of the natural island.
