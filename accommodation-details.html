Accommodation

Villa Nima is fully EOT licensed with the Greek National Tourist Organisation to ensure user safety and compliance.

Ornate designed gates greet you as you arrive at the Villa with adequate parking facility for at least two cars. Stone pathways and landscaped garden lead you to the Villa entrance.

Inside the ground floor open plan area is furnished to a high quality c/w 2 large 3 seater leather sofas, Natural oak dining suite, besoke wine rack, Modern 37" flat screen, DVD and I Pod/ CD Music system. FREE Unlimited WIFI Access.

The kitchen area is fully fitted with modern appliances and is fully kitted out with all cookware and utensils you may need. Around the Villa there are five intimate 2 seater bistro tables and a unique slate round table which will seat 4.

The sun terrace has 8 sun loungers. The pool is beautifully tiled in sky blue mosaic tiles and is illuminated. Adjacent to the pool there are stone steps which take you to the private landscaped garden.

Within the garden area there is a traditional designed pergola c/w 2 round tables and 8 chairs. This is all adjacent to the traditional stone BBQ.
